Bio

Dr Xiaohan Yu took up the role of Lecturer in Artificial Intelligence (AI) at Macquarie University’s School of Computing in December 2023. Before that, he completed his doctoral studies at Griffith University, Australia, and joined as a Research Fellow the ARC Research Hub for Driving Farming Productivity and Disease Prevention (Griffith University). He received the APRS (Australian Pattern Recognition Society) ECR Award 2022 for his contribution in bridging pattern recognition and smart farming. Dr Yu’s research interests include Ultra-Fine-Grained Visual Categorization (Ultra-FGVC), Smart Farming, and Automated Crop Cultivar Identification. He established and pushed forward the research progress of Ultra-FGVC, and authored over 60 (over 30 with CORE A* ranking) publications in reputable conferences and journals including ICCV, CVPR, ECCV, IEEE Transactions on Pattern Analysis and Machine Intelligence, International Journal of Computer Vision, AAAI, IJCAI, Pattern Recognition, IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systems, IEEE Transactions on Circuits and Systems for Video Technology, etc. He is the Associate Editor of journal Pattern Recognition and SN Computer Science, and Area Chair for ACM MM 2024&2025, IJCNN2025, and SPC for IJCAI2025. He received Outstanding Area Chair of ACM Multimedia 2024.

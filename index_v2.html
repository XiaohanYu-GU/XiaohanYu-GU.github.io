About

I am a Lecturer in Artificial Intelligence at Macquarie University with research interests in computer vision, machine learning, and smart agriculture. I specialize in ultra-fine-grained visual categorization, representation learning, and domain adaptation. My work aims to bridge AI with real-world impact, particularly in sustainable agriculture.

Research

  • Ultra-Fine-Grained Visual Categorization: Created the largest dataset platform for cultivar classification [ICCV'21]
  • Efficient Representation Learning: Developed Lie algebra-based self-supervised methods for efficient model training [PR'23]
  • Cross-Domain AI: Focus on domain-aware continual learning for agriculture deployment [Pattern Recognition, AAAI, CVPR]

Selected Publications

  • Self-supervised Learning in Lie Algebra for Discriminative Visual Embedding. Pattern Recognition, 2023.
  • Ultra-FGVC: Cultivar Classification at Scale. ICCV, 2021.
  • Part-based Vision Transformers for Plant Recognition. Pattern Recognition, 2022.
